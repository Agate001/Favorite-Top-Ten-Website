d-block #10 One Punch Man

Saitama is OP mainly due to the “Power of the Pen” but it does have a lot of humor in the series and you can see the characters around him grow and its mostly just a funny series to watch that also has some great action in it. It’s not super kid friendly but it’s silly enough that even kids like the humor in the show.

#9 The Devil is a Part-Timer!

This series is a funny slice of life from the point of view of the “Devil” after losing his powers and having to work at MgRonalds (a parody of McDonalds) and dealing with demons and angels trying to destroy his new life. The action is fun and allows for most kids to watch without much worry. The story is charming and mostly safe for kids.

#8 Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is not a suitable pick for kids due to extremely gory and violent scenes, including intense battles, dismemberment, and humans being eaten by Titans but the story is amazing and complex. The titans and the fight scenes are fantastic and show off just how far animation has come. Great use of CGI along with an amazing story.

#7 Solo Leveling

The titans and the fight scenes are fantastic and show off just how far animation has come. Great use of CGI along with an amazing story.

#6 SpyXFamily

Loid has to create a family in order to try to stop a possible war. has to marry, adopt a child and have that child enroll in the nations most prestigious school in order to get close to his target. His adopted child Anya has telepathic powers and his wife Yor is a secret assassin. The story is a slice of life, comedy, with action while being family friendly.

#5 My Hero Academia

Deku’s story about how he became the greatest Hero in the world even though he was born with no powers and had to “inherit” his mentors powers. Funny, mostly kid friendly, unique characters and amazing story. Each character has an arch no matter how small a role they play in the grand scheme of things making it a diverse cast.

#4 Naruto

Naruto is hands down one of the best anime stories of all time. He has one of the best story arcs in all of anime going from goofball loner to village hero by sticking to his moral code as much as possible. Family friendly for the most part although there can be some dark tones but its action packed and funny.

#3 Full Metal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Edward and Alphonse Elric attempt human transmutation and pay dearly for attempting to bring their mother back from the dead. The series is very dark but has a ton of humor and action without being overly gory. It is a story about the bonds of family that try to sacrifice for one another out of love.

#2 Demon Slayer

Tanjiro is such a great character as he is very friendly and optimistic and it seems to rub off on everyone he meets. His family is attacked by a demon when he was away and he comes home to find his sister is now a demon. He must train to kill the king of demons in the hopes of returning her to her original form.

#1 Dragon Ball Z/Super

The first anime that most kids watched in the US as it was shown on Cartoon Network. Goku is an alien that comes to earth and becomes its protector along with the other Z Warriors who have to save us from galactic threats. Comedic, action packed, and with lots of character development. Best Anime ever.
